# About Maa Sewa Healthcare

## Who We Are

Maa Sewa Healthcare is a professional home healthcare company headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Founded with the mission to bring dignity and hospital-grade medical expertise into the homes of patients, Maa Sewa Healthcare has served hundreds of families across Pune and Mumbai since its founding in 2022.

The name "Maa Sewa" — meaning "Mother's Care" in Hindi — reflects the organization's philosophy: providing care that is as attentive, warm, and professional as the care a mother would provide.

## What We Do

Maa Sewa Healthcare specializes in six core home healthcare services:

**Home Nursing:** We deploy qualified GNM (General Nursing & Midwifery) and B.Sc Nursing-certified nurses to patients' homes for 8-hour, 12-hour, or 24-hour shifts. These nurses handle clinical duties including wound dressing, IV administration, vital monitoring, medication management, and coordination with treating physicians.

**ICU Home Setup:** For critically ill patients who are stable enough to leave the hospital but require intensive monitoring, Maa Sewa Healthcare establishes a fully equipped ICU-grade environment at home. This includes ventilators, cardiac monitors, oxygen concentrators, BiPAP/CPAP machines, and infusion pumps — all managed by ICU-trained nursing staff around the clock.

**Elder Care:** Our dedicated caregivers support senior citizens with daily living activities including mobility assistance, personal hygiene, medication reminders, nutritional support, and companionship. Our elder care program is built on the principles of dignity, safety, and emotional wellbeing.

**Post-Operative Care:** The weeks after surgery are critical. Maa Sewa Healthcare provides specialized post-surgical nursing including sterile wound care, surgical site infection prevention, pain management, drain management, and early physiotherapy to prevent complications like DVT.

**Specialized Care:** For complex neurological and terminal conditions, our trained caregivers and nurses provide stroke rehabilitation support, dementia and Alzheimer's care using evidence-based behavioral management techniques, and compassionate palliative and end-of-life care.

**Injection & IV Visit:** For patients who need a single clinical procedure — an IM/IV injection, IV drip, blood sample collection, or adult vaccination — our nurses make home visits, typically arriving within 45 minutes in Pune and Mumbai.

## How We Ensure Quality

Every nurse and caregiver at Maa Sewa Healthcare goes through a rigorous vetting process before being assigned to any patient:

1. **Clinical Verification:** Degree certificates (GNM or B.Sc Nursing) are verified with the Indian Nursing Council.
2. **Police Verification:** A formal police background check is conducted for every staff member.
3. **Internal Training:** All staff undergo Maa Sewa's internal clinical training covering ICU protocols, emergency response, sterile technique, and specialized equipment handling.
4. **Supervisor Audits:** Senior nursing supervisors conduct periodic in-person visits to audit the quality of care provided at each patient's home.
5. **Replacement Guarantee:** If a patient or family is not satisfied with the assigned nurse for any reason, a replacement is provided within 24 hours at no additional cost.
